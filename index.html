Alexej Behnisch

Professional

I am a political scientist responsible for Governmental Relations at the CISPA Helmholtz Center for Information Security, Germany’s leading research institution for cybersecurity, privacy and trustworthy AI.

Responsibilities at CISPA cover the full spectrum of political work, from political communications and public affairs, including international and scientific cooperation, to political monitoring, risk analysis and policy expertise in the cyber and digital domain.

An expert in international relations with degrees from Aberystwyth and the London School of Economics (LSE) as well as study visits at the University of Heidelberg and the University of Southern California (USC), I worked in the past as a researcher, teacher, writer, editor and political advisor across Europe, with longer stays in Britain and Portugal.
